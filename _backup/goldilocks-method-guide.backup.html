1. The Real Risk Investors Face

For short-term rental investors, the scariest scenario isn't overspending.
It's having a property that doesn't cash flow because it fails to book.

  • Every month under-booked = you still pay mortgage, taxes, insurance, utilities, and maintenance.
  • A single year of weak performance can erase years of equity gains.

That's why design isn't just about aesthetics. Done strategically, it's a risk management tool that keeps your property reliably booked — so it at least pays for itself.
