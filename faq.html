Frequently Asked Questions

What to know before starting a project with 1584 Design.

About Our Services

What does "full-service" interior design mean?

We handle everything from design planning through procurement and installation. You don't source a single item, coordinate a single delivery, or manage a single vendor. We present the plan, you approve it, and we execute it completely.

Learn more about how this works for vacation rentals or second homes.

Do you do renovations or remodels?

No. We furnish and accessorize existing spaces — furniture, rugs, curtains, art, lighting fixtures, bedding, and accessories. We do not do construction, demolition, or structural changes.

Do you design primary residences?

No. We specialize in vacation rentals and second homes exclusively. Primary residences involve different constraints — existing furniture, daily-use compromises, more change requests — that don't align with how we work best.

What areas do you serve?

Our home market is St. George and the Zion corridor in Southern Utah. We also serve Las Vegas and take select projects nationally.

Process

How long does a project take?

Most projects take 8–16 weeks from design kickoff to installation, depending on scope and lead times for custom pieces.

How involved do I need to be?

Minimally. We present a complete design plan for your approval. Beyond a few decision points, the process is designed to be hands-off. Most clients prefer it that way.

What's the first step?

A quick call — a conversation about your property, goals, and timeline. From there we'll tell you honestly whether we're a good fit and what the project would look like. Request a call to get started.

For STR Investors

What makes STR design different from regular interior design?

STR design is performance-driven. Every choice — layout, furnishings, photography angles, durability — serves three goals: stop the scroll on listing sites, convert browsers to bookers, and create five-star guest experiences.

Regular interior design optimizes for the owner's taste. STR design optimizes for revenue.

What size properties do you work with?

We specialize in large-group properties accommodating 8+ guests. These are the properties where design has the biggest impact on revenue — more bedrooms, more common areas, more opportunity to differentiate.

Can you help with listing photography?

We design with photography in mind. Our spaces are built to photograph well from specific angles and in specific light. We don't shoot the photos ourselves, but we design knowing that the listing photos are the first thing that matters.

For Second Home Owners

How do you make a second home feel personal, not generic?

Through our Intentional Home Framework — we align the design with three layers: the environment outside the windows, the architecture of the home, and the identity of the owner.

A home designed to be everything equally is distinctive in none of it. Learn more about our approach.

Can I keep some of my existing furniture?

We start from scratch. Our process works best when we design a complete, cohesive space. Incorporating existing pieces creates compromises that dilute the result. If there are truly meaningful heirloom pieces, we can discuss it — but our default is a clean slate.
